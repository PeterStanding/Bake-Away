Frequently Aasked Questions

What is BakeAway?

BakeAway delivery service is an on-demand pastry delivery application which allows people to order from a selection of local bakeries through a click of a button.Our aim is to revolutionise the food delivery industry by introducing a fast, transparent, and reliable ordering software.We bring you local pastries from the places that you know and love.

How does it work?

Simply enter your address , select the bakery and begin choosing the pastry your heart desires.

At what times can I place a order?

Different bakeries will have different opening times, but we deliver from morning till 7pm

Is there a minimum spend?

depending on the bakery you are ordering from but you will be informed at check out.

What if I'm handed the wrong order?

we understand that sometimes things might go wrong. If this is the case, contact our customer service team and report any issues, for further information please go to the customer service section.

What if I have allergies?

If you have specific allergies and are concerned about any items on a menu, please contact the bakery directly.

What if my order is late?

Sometimes things outside of a rider’s control can cause a delay. We will always try and proactively call you if we become aware that your order might not arrive within the estimated time of delivery, we will work something out.

What if I’m not around when my rider arrives?

If you think you won’t be at the delivery address in time to receive your order, please contact customer service.

Your rider will always try to call you if there’s an issue once they reach your delivery address. If they’re unable to contact you, our Customer Service team will try and reach you via phone and email. Please check your emails during the delivery time for any updates.
